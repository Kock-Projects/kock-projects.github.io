MusicDJ

MusicDJ is an old application included on old Sony Ericsson phones which let you made tracks based on pre-defined sounds. The tracks were saved on a .midi format, meaning what you could finish editing them on a PC before converting them to MP3. Unfortunately, when Sony Ericsson was converted into Sony Mobile Communications, this appliaction was forgotten.

Our plans

HG managed to get all the sounds from the application, to start re-creating this application, for PC. Njifra will code it starting with a basic design, then styles will be added. But what really matters is that the application can play the audio.
